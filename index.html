About Us

We help investors find the true value of stocks using the intrinsic value and margin of safety method popularized by Warren Buffett. Our tool offers two options to calculate the intrinsic value: Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) and Multiples.

Features

Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)

Calculate the intrinsic value of a stock using future cash flows discounted back to their present value.

Multiples

Estimate the intrinsic value based on a multiple of a financial metric, such as earnings or sales.
